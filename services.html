Special Services

Our cardiology department provides expert care using advanced diagnostic tools and treatments. Our dedicated team focuses on prevention, early detection, and personalized therapies to enhance heart health and overall quality of life.

Oncology focuses on diagnosing, treating, and managing cancer. Our department provides comprehensive care with advanced diagnostics, innovative treatments, and personalized support, helping patients through every step of their cancer journey.

Neurology focuses on disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Our department provides expert care for conditions such as headaches, epilepsy, stroke, and neurodegenerative diseases, using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatments to enhance patients' neurological health and quality of life.

Orthopedics focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions of the bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments. Our department offers care for sports injuries, fractures, arthritis, and joint replacements, using advanced techniques to restore mobility and enhance quality of life.

Urology deals with cond itions of the urinary tract and male reproductive system. We offer expert care for issues like kidney stones, urinary incontinence, and prostate disorders, using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatments to enhance urological health.

Dermatology treats conditions of the skin, hair, and nails. Our department provides expert care for acne, eczema, skin cancer, and cosmetic concerns, using advanced treatments to enhance skin health and overall well-being.

Pediatrics focuses on the health and development of children from infancy to adolescence. Our department offers comprehensive care, including check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for various health issues, with a commitment to compassionate, age-appropriate care for every child.

Gynecology focuses on female reproductive health. Our department offers expert care for routine exams, menstrual issues, pregnancy concerns, and menopausal symptoms, with a commitment to personalized and compassionate care for women at all life stages.

Gastroenterology specializes in digestive system disorders. Our department offers expert care for issues like acid reflux, ulcers, and liver disease, using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatments to enhance digestive health and well-being.

Pulmonology deals with respiratory conditions. Our department offers expert care for asthma, COPD, and lung infections, using advanced diagnostics and treatments to enhance lung health and quality of life.

Our health awareness campaigns aim to educate and empower the community about important health issues. Through workshops, screenings, and informational resources, we strive to promote preventive care, improve health literacy, and encourage healthy lifestyle choices for a healthier community.

E-channeling

eChanneling is an online platform that allows patients to easily book medical appointments with doctors and specialists at hospitals. It enables patients to search for available doctors by specialty, hospital, or location, and schedule appointments quickly, reducing waiting times and enhancing satisfaction.
